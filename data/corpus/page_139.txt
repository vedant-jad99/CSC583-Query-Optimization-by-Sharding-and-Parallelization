Other versions of the theorem involving the weak or strong energy condition also exist.
In modified gravity, the Einstein field equations do not hold and so these singularities do not
necessarily arise. For example, in Infinite Derivative Gravity, it is possible for 
 to be negative
even if the Null Energy Condition holds.[8][9]
